Wade "DeadPool" Wilson aka "Merc with the Mouth"

Here's an ability list of DeadPool:

  • Enhanced Strength - Through the Ajax program, his physical abilities as a human has been enhanced.
  • Enhanced Speed - He's faster than the average human.
  • Enhanced Resilience - Higher endurance and tolerance for pain. Possibly no sensation for pain.
  • Enhanced Agility - High mobility, balance and body coordination allows Deadpool to perform amazing acrobatic feats.
  • Enhanced Stamina - DeadPool's healing factor allows him to exert himself beyond human limits. Almost little to no signs of fatigue.
  • Enhanced Reflexes - Adept at melee and Close Quarters Combat. Able to deflect bullets with ease and catch axes thrown by Francis.
  • Healing Factor - Comparable to Wolverine's Healing Factor but Enhanced. Allows him to heal within minutes from deathblows. Possible immortality.
